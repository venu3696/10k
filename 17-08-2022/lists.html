Chicken Pizza

Pizza Dough

  1. Instant Yeast- 1 tsp
  2. Flour- 3 cup
  3. Salt- 3/4 tsp
  4. Sugar- 1 tsp

Method 1

  • First off all put yeast in 4 tsp warm water and make frontly, then mix all dry ingredients
    and then mix yeast and make a dough with warm water, put aside until it has doubled in size.

Pizza Sauce

  • Onion- 1 medium
  • Garlic-1 1 clove
  • Oil- 3 tsp
  • Tomatoes-5
  • Red crushed chill- 1/2 tsp
  • Salt- as taste
  • Tomato ketchup- 2 tsp
  • Oregano- 1/4 tsp

Method 2

  • Take a pan, put oil and heat it up and put thiny chopped onion and garlic and fry until soft.
  • Then put chopped tomatoes , ted crushed chili, salt, tomato ketchip and doregano and mix well and let it cook till tomato mix well and become in tomato sauce form. if you need water. put a bit of water and cook until done.
  • When done apply in pizza base

Pizza Topping

  1. Chicken- 30%
  2. Oil- 2 tsp
  3. Garlic-1 clove
  4. Lemon juice- 2 tbsp
  5. Salt - as taste
  6. Mushrooms- as you like
  7. Red chili powder- 2 tsp
  8. Mozzarella cheese- as you need
  9. Oregano- 1%
  10. Onion- 1
  11. Capsicum

Method 3

  • Take a pan put oil and heat it up. then put chichen and garlic and fry till chichem becomes in white color. then put lemon juice and salt and mox well.
  • When water is dry put mushrooms and fry for 1min and take it off the cooker, let it cool, then put cheese. red chili powder and oregano and mix well.
  • On a floured surface, roll out diugh and place on a greased sheet pan
  • Shape to fill the sheet pan, spread pizza sauce evenly over pizza dough
  • Bake for 12 minutes or until cheese bubbles in preheated oven 240C. 490F, and Gas mark 9.
